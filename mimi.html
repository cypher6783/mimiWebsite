Our Philosophy

Clothing is
not worn.
It is inhabited.

At Mimi's Stitches, we believe the finest garments are not purchased — they are co-authored. Every consultation is a dialogue between craft and character, between needle and narrative.

We work exclusively with a select clientele who understand that true luxury is not the price tag — it is the irreplaceable feeling of wearing something made precisely, and only, for you.

Craft Without Compromise

The Maker Behind The Mark
Mimi
Founder & Creative Director

"I did not choose tailoring. Tailoring chose me — in a room full of fabric, with a needle in my hand and a vision I could not yet name."

Over twelve years, Mimi has built an atelier rooted in a singular conviction: that clothing is the most intimate form of architecture. Every garment she designs begins with a question — not "what do you want to wear?" but "who do you want to become?"

Trained in classical European tailoring techniques and deeply informed by African textile heritage, Mimi occupies a rare intersection: rigorous craft and fearless design. Her work has dressed brides, executives, cultural icons, and those who simply refuse to be dressed by anyone else.

The Process

From first
word to
final stitch.

Every commission follows a considered sequence — unhurried, collaborative, and centered entirely on you.

01

The Consultation

A private appointment to discuss your vision, occasion, lifestyle, and aesthetic identity. This is a conversation — not a sales encounter.

02

Measurement & Fabric Selection

Precise measurements taken across 24 reference points. Fabrics selected from our curated mill library — shown and explained in detail.

03

Pattern & Construction

A unique pattern drafted for your body. The garment is cut and constructed by hand — canvas floating free, seams pressed and shaped.

04

The Fittings

Two to three fittings ensure the garment moves with you — not on you. Every adjustment made with surgical precision until the fit is irreproachable.

05

Delivery & Legacy

Your garment is delivered with care instructions and a record of your pattern — so every future commission begins from a place of already knowing you.

Everything You Need to Know

Your questions,
answered.

How does the consultation process work?
+
Our consultations are private, appointment-only sessions held at the atelier. We begin with a conversation about your vision, occasion, and aesthetic identity — no pressure, no sales agenda. The consultation typically lasts 60–90 minutes and forms the creative foundation of your commission.
What is the pricing structure?
+
Pricing is determined by garment complexity, fabric selection, and production time. Each commission is quoted individually after the initial consultation — this ensures you receive an accurate, transparent figure with no hidden costs. Bridal gowns, bespoke suits, and alteration projects are each priced differently. Contact us for a consultation to discuss your specific requirements.
How long does production take?
+
Standard bespoke commissions require 6–10 weeks from confirmation to delivery. Bridal gowns require a minimum of 12–16 weeks to allow for multiple fittings and handwork. We recommend initiating your commission as early as possible — rush timelines are accommodated selectively and subject to availability.
How early should bridal orders be placed?
+
We recommend placing bridal orders a minimum of 5–6 months before the wedding date. This allows sufficient time for design development, fabric procurement, construction, and multiple fittings without any compromise to the work. For particularly complex gowns, 8–10 months is advised.
Can I request completely custom designs?
+
Every commission at Mimi's Stitches is custom by definition. We do not work from catalogues or pre-set designs — your garment is conceived, designed, and constructed entirely around your requirements. You may bring reference images, sketches, or simply a feeling, and we will build from there.
Do you handle alterations on existing garments?
+
Yes — we approach alterations with the same rigour as new commissions. Whether restoring a vintage piece, adjusting a beloved garment, or reimagining something entirely, we bring craft and intention to every alteration. An assessment appointment is required before any alteration work commences.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Yes. We work with clients across Africa, Europe, and internationally. For international commissions, we conduct initial consultations virtually, arrange a dedicated in-person measurement and fitting session during your visit, and coordinate the remaining fittings around your schedule. Garments are delivered securely via courier or collected in person.
How many clients do you accept per season?
+
We deliberately limit new commissions each season to ensure every client receives our full, undivided attention. When our commission calendar is full, we operate a waiting list. We recommend reaching out early — particularly for bridal and major event commissions — to secure your place in our schedule.
